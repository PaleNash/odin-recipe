    Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 200g Lean Beef Mince
  • 50g Shredded Cheddar
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Zuchini
  • Herbs: Basil, Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper

    Instructions

  1. Shred carrot and zuchini
  2. Combine Beef mince with shredded carroy and zuchini in large pan over medium heat, cook untill meat is browned
  3. Once browned add tomato puree, basil, oregano, salt and pepper
  4. Simmer at medium heat for 15 minutes, then reduce to low
  5. Boil pot of water, once boiled combine with Spaghetti for 3-5 minutes untill 'el dente'
  6. Drain spaghetti and serve with meat mixture, and shredded cheese over the top
